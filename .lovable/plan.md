## 9 Square —  Square your life

A modern, visually rich multi-page website for **9 Square**, an authorized Godrej Interio furniture dealer. The site will use **Godrej Interio's brand colors** (cream pink #ff5c56, white `#fffff`, and warm creams) throughout.

### Pages & Structure

**1. Home Page**

- Full-width hero banner with a bold tagline like "Square your life" and a CTA button
- Animated product category cards (Office, Home, Storage, Godrej Security) with hover effects
- "Why Choose Us" section with icons (Authorized Dealer badge, warranty, delivery, installation)
- Featured products carousel with images
- Client logos marquee/scroll section
- Footer with contact info, quick links, social media

**2. Products Page**

- Filter tabs: Office Furniture, Home Furniture, Storage Solutions, Godrej Storage
- Product grid with cards showing image, name, brief description, and "Enquire Now" button
- Each category will have 6-8 sample product images (using high-quality Unsplash furniture images)

**3. About Us Page**

- Company story, mission, and Godrej Interio partnership highlight
- Team or showroom image section
- Key stats (years in business, happy clients, products delivered)

**4. Clients Page**

- Grid of placeholder client logos with company names
- Testimonials section with placeholder quotes

**5. Contact Page**

- Contact form (Name, Email, Phone, Message)
- Store address, phone, email, working hours
- Embedded map placeholder

### Design Approach

- **Godrej Interio brand palette**: Deep blue primary, green accents, clean whites
- Modern card-based layouts with subtle shadows and rounded corners
- Smooth scroll animations and hover effects
- Fully responsive (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- Professional navigation bar with logo area and mobile hamburger menu
- Consistent footer across all pages

### Navigation

- Sticky top navbar: Home | Products | About Us | Clients | Contact
- Mobile: hamburger menu with slide-in drawer
